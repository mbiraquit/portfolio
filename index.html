Project Spotlight: IoT-BASED AUTOMATED HYDROPONIC NUTRIENT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM


My journey in the field culminated in our award-winning thesis: "The IoT-Based Automated Hydroponic Nutrient Solution Management System." This groundbreaking study elevates traditional hydroponic systems by seamlessly integrating sensors for continuous monitoring and adjustment of crucial TDS and pH values. Our thesis was honored with multiple accolades, securing the prestigious 2nd place for Best Thesis in our department. Its excellence was further recognized as a participant in the Best Undergraduate Thesis competition within our college and as a finalist in the esteemed Outstanding Student Creative Research (Sibol Award) under the Northern Luzon chapter of DOST-TAPI. This academic achievement embodies my dedication to pushing technological boundaries and finding practical solutions that merge innovation with real-world impact. The project not only demonstrates my technical prowess but also showcases my ability to collaborate effectively within a team, navigate complex challenges, and deliver tangible results. My aspiration to contribute meaningfully to the technology landscape is rooted in experiences like these, where innovation meets practicality.
